## üéØ Mission
Hope With Bitcoin is a humanitarian initiative committed to **feeding, supporting, and educating vulnerable communities** in Benin, leveraging **Bitcoin and Nostr** as tools for financial inclusion and empowerment.

### Our Goals
1. **Food Assistance:** Provide regular meals to street children, families in need, and isolated elderly.  
2. **Institutional Support:** Supply orphanages and public schools with essential resources.  
3. **Financial Education (‚Çø):** Teach beneficiaries about Bitcoin and Nostr.  
4. **Community Awareness:** Run public educational campaigns with practical demos, stickers, and satoshi payments.
